Introduction

Rizee is a India based online education platform created to help higher secondary students to inculcate optimal time management methods while preparing for competitive national exams i.e. NEET and JEE. Rizee empowers students to prepare for the exams in a playful yet effective manner that includes reflecting and reassessing the progress in the syllabus from time to time.

Rizee also provides a tool for educational institutions that is intended specifically to help teachers create question papers that can be used for online tests via App and also keep track of their students progress efficiently.

Project Objectives

In today’s competitive digital world, students find it difficult to keep track of their study progress and manage time while preparing for exams. Rizee focuses on providing affordable and quality online education and creating better study experience with gamification of practice to make learning a fun process.
