Akash Raje. Hi, I'm Akash

A user experience designer currently working at Dafter Inc.

Technology always fascinates me and Art gives inspiration. I pursued my graduation in Animation & visual design and worked in a VFX industry for 2 years. It was quite a fun to work on Hollywood and Netflix projects, later on I discovered I really want to create products that solve real life problems and make an impact on lives.

During my school days I realized, the everyday things that we take for granted are in fact carefully designed to make our lives easier. Every decade represents a different era of unique gadgets and along with that appear the issues of usability, This is where I find the opportunity to establish myself as a problem solver with the focus on of Human Centered Design.
‍
Interaction Design Foundation and Google's UX Design programs have helped me deep dive into the field of Experience design. Also the projects that I worked on have enabled me in prioritizing user needs while taking design decisions and it just feels amazing when I as a designer help people by creating experiences that offer them legit solutions.
